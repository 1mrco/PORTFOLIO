## Quick Fix - Remove Password Protection

Your Vercel deployment might have password protection enabled. Here's how to make it public:

### Step 1: Go to Vercel Dashboard
Visit: **https://vercel.com/1mrcos-projects/portfolo/settings/deployment-protection**

### Step 2: Disable Password Protection
1. Find **"Deployment Protection"** section
2. Look for **"Password Protection"** or **"Vercel Authentication"**
3. **Disable** or **Turn Off** password protection
4. Click **"Save"**

### Step 3: Alternative - Check Project Settings
1. Go to: **https://vercel.com/1mrcos-projects/portfolo/settings**
2. Look for **"Privacy"** or **"Access Control"**
3. Make sure it's set to **"Public"**
4. Save changes

### Step 4: Redeploy (if needed)
After changing settings, you may need to:
1. Go to **Deployments** tab
2. Click **"..."** on latest deployment
3. Click **"Redeploy"**

## Your Public URLs:
- **Main:** https://portfolo-alpha.vercel.app
- **Alternative:** https://portfolo-1mrcos-projects.vercel.app

After disabling password protection, these URLs will be accessible to everyone!
